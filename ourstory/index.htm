Our Story

We know that coffee is about so much more than the drink that’s in your cup. Coffee is connected to memories: the morning before starting a new job, a late night java run during finals week, something to keep you warm during the kids’ soccer practice. Each cup of Javi’s Coffee is loving crafted to keep you going. Wherever you are and whatever you’re doing, we’re with you to help see it through.

Responsible Sourcing

We work directly with our farmers and their communities to ensure that we can make a positive impact where it matters most. We invest heavily in ensuring that we’re able to trace our beans back to the farm where they were grown and empower farmers to grow their operations with access to this data. We are committed to supporting our farmers in their mission to grow and process coffee beans sustainably.

In 2019, we reached our goal of sourcing 100% of our coffee ethically and are committed to continually improving our responsible sourcing efforts in the future.
