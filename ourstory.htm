Our Story

Javi Ramirez went on a quest to craft a really delicious cup of coffee. He wanted to create a blend that was filled with flavor, complex, and smooth. After traveling the world, Javi is proud to present you with 13 of his discoveries.

Welcome to Javi's, we think you'll like it here.

Javi's Coffee

We source and roast the very best sustainably-sourced beans from around the world. Since 2019, we have served our local community and have had two goals: share our coffee with the community and make the world a better place. With each and every cup, we invite you to share in this mission.
